hw4

Georeference a historical image from USGS Earth Explorer

  • Watch the tutorial videos on how to georeference an image, download an image from USGS Earth Explorer
  • Select an areal photo single frame at a scale larger than 1:30,000 from an area of interest (besides Chapel Hill). You should be interested in seeing how this area has changed through time.
  • Georeference the historical image that you selected, for this hw it will be ok to use the ESRI tileset as the reference, however that is usually not the ideal.
  • Save a control point layer with at least 6 control points.

What will you turn in before the deadline?

  1. On Canvas, upload a PNG map that contains the georeferenced historical image and attach your control points (or copy and paste them to the Canvas text box). The map should have your name, a title and a scale. We should be able to see your newly georeferenced image (with some transparency) over your base map or othro image.
