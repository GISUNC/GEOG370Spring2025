By the end of this section you should be able to:
- Explain the terms georeferencing, basemap, and control points.
- Understand the applications of georeferencing historical imagery.
- Recognize what makes a good control point, depending on the scale and composition of the image being georeferenced.
- Georeference an image that has no geographic references using QGIS or ARCGIS.
- Understand what means to stretch, scale, rotate and skew the image.
- Distinguish the different distortions caused by different transformation models.
